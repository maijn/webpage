About me

I AM
MAI JULIE. 21.


I travel to destinations with a story to tell.
I travel to destinations with hidden gems.
I travel to destinations with local specialities and jaw-dropping natural scenery.


Travel and photography are my escape from my normal life.

As a wanderlust, I try to be a slow-traveller; to live and fall in love with every culture I come across.

I AM FROM
NORWAY


Living in a small beautiful country in Europe with multicultural background, makes me excited about differenct cultures, behaviors and perspectivs.


My desire for traveling and learning new cultures sparked at my start of the twenties. As a full-time student, it's difficult to travel with a thin wallet, but this problem won't stop me from exploring other countries. Since travel and photography are my passion, I have worked hard to get these opportunities to travel all these countries.

"Seize the day"

Thanks to my course DM3005, I got the opportunity to make this blog and document my travels. I will share my stories and photography on my blog.

In doing so, I hope to foster a richer understanding of others and increase awareness how we can travel better, one journey at a time.
